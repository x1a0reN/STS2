Frequently Asked Questions

How many cards are in Slay the Spire 2?
There are currently 575 unique cards in the Slay the Spire 2 database, including 503 collectible cards that can appear in card rewards and shops. The remaining 72 are non-collectible cards: 15 Status cards, 18 Curse cards, and special Event/Token/Quest cards.
How many cards does each character have in STS2?
Each of the five playable characters has a comparable card pool: Ironclad (87), Silent (88), Defect (88), Necrobinder (88), and Regent (88). Additionally, there are 64 Colorless cards available to all characters.
What are the card types in Slay the Spire 2?
Cards are divided into three main types: Attacks (197 cards) deal damage, Skills (230 cards) provide Block and utility, and Powers (112 cards) grant persistent combat effects. There are also Status (15), Curse (18), and Quest (3) cards.
What are Power cards in Slay the Spire 2?
Power cards provide persistent effects for the entire combat once played. Unlike Attacks and Skills, Power cards are removed from your deck for the rest of the fight. There are currently 112 Power cards across all characters.
What are the card rarities in Slay the Spire 2?
There are five rarity tiers: Basic/Starter (19), Common (100), Uncommon (220), Rare (155), and Ancient (18). Higher rarity cards have stronger effects but appear less frequently in card rewards.
How many playable characters are in Slay the Spire 2?
There are 5 playable characters: Ironclad, Silent, Defect, Necrobinder, and Regent. Each has a distinct card pool and playstyle.
