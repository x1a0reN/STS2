Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best relics in Slay the Spire 2?

Based on community Elo data from over 1028+ recorded runs, the highest-rated Ancient Relics are Arcane Scroll, Lead Paperweight, Small Capsule. These S-Tier relics are consistently chosen by players when offered in the pick-one-of-three Ancient selection.

How do Ancient Relics work in Slay the Spire 2?

Ancient Relics are obtained from Ancients — powerful beings encountered at the start of each Act. Neow always appears in Act 1, Orobas or Pael in Act 2, and Vakuu in Act 3. Each Ancient offers 3 random relics from their pool, and you must pick one. These relics are typically high-impact and can define an entire run.

What is the Elo rating for Slay the Spire 2 relics?

Elo ratings measure how often a relic is chosen over alternatives in the pick-one-of-three Ancient relic selection. A higher Elo means players consistently prefer that relic. The average Elo is 1500. Elo reflects player preference, not necessarily win rate — some high-Elo relics have moderate win rates because they are picked in difficult situations.
