# Scale Rules

Use these limits to prevent design inflation.

## 3-day demo

- 1 core loop
- at most 1 secondary loop
- at most 3 screens
- at most 1 enemy/event family
- no heavy narrative branching

## 2-week web prototype

- 1 core loop
- at most 2 secondary loops
- at most 6 screens
- at most 2 content families
- one simple progression layer

## 1-month playable prototype

- 1 core loop
- at most 3 secondary loops
- at most 10 screens
- at most 3 content families
- one stronger progression layer

## 1-3 month small commercial prototype

- 1 core loop
- at most 3 secondary loops
- at most 12-15 screens
- at most 4 content families
- stronger meta progression allowed

If the requested design exceeds the scale, cut systems before adding polish.
